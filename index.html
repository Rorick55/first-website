Why choose me?

           From what I understand, the teachers at Launch Academy are looking to invest their knowledge and experience in driven individuals. They are not looking for people who are only interested in a quick career option. They want people who will take all that experience they have passed on and use it to build something beautiful. These people don't create because they have to, they do it because they love every moment they spend creating. With every fiber of my being I would like to prove to Launch Academy that I am this kind of investment.
          Although I am quite new to the computer science world, I cannot remember the last time I was so passionately driven to dedicate all my time to one thing. I feel like Harry Potter during his first year of Hogwarts. It's as if there is a whole world I have never known, filled with magic and potential beyond my wildest dreams, and now I get to emerse myself in it's wonder.
          The only problem is that on my own there is only so much I can learn. I would change my major, but the longer I stay in
college and watch my graduated friends struggle to find jobs, the
more I realize the current college system is broken. This is where
Launch Academy comes in. Not only do I firmly believe that their
education system is the future of education, but they also
focus on my newfound passion of coding. I understand this will
not be a walk in the park, but I am a very hardworking individual
and I know I will be a great addition to the Launch Academy
classroom.
          Thank you Launch Academy for taking me into consideration
for a spot in your summer session. I hope someday I get the chance
to prove my value to everyone at Launch Academy.
